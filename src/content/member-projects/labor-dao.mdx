## About Labor DAO

Labor DAO is a global decentralized community supporting workers who want to organize, strike, and collectively bargain. Built on the principles of worker solidarity and decentralized coordination, Labor DAO aims to be the first community of its kind to leverage blockchain technology for labor organizing.

## Purpose

To create a decentralized infrastructure for worker solidarity, enabling workers worldwide to organize, coordinate collective action, and bargain for better conditions using Web3 tools and community-driven governance.

---

## QUESTIONS!

### How do you plan to incorporate BREAD in your org?

Labor DAO plans to integrate BREAD as part of its ecosystem, enabling workers and organizers to transact and coordinate using the Bread Cooperative's community currency.

### What do you plan to do in the short term with the funds received from Bread Cooperative?

Funds would support the development of Labor DAO's infrastructure for worker organizing and community building.

### What processes do you have to determine how funds from Bread Cooperative are allocated?

Labor DAO follows a community-driven governance model where proposals are shared for feedback and ratified by DAO members.
