## About Gardens

Gardens is a conviction-based funding platform that allows communities to fund projects through sustained support. Members allocate voting power to proposals they believe in, with support growing stronger over time.

## Bread Cooperative Integration

The Bread Cooperative Community Initiatives Pool on Gardens supports projects that advance post-capitalist values and solidarity economics. BREAD token holders can participate in conviction-based voting to fund community-led initiatives.
